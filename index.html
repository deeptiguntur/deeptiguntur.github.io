04 — Research

Publications

ACL 2026 · Student Research Workshop Peer Reviewed

Test-Time Strategies for More Efficient and Accurate Agentic RAG

Adobe Research
×
UMass Amherst

Agentic Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems iteratively retrieve and reason over external documents to answer complex, multi-hop questions — but often struggle to effectively utilize retrieved context, resulting in redundant retrieval operations and high inference cost. This paper introduces test-time strategies, including a memory cache module that reuses previously retrieved context across reasoning steps, reducing unnecessary retrievals while preserving answer quality. We evaluate using LLM-as-a-Judge on standard multi-hop QA benchmarks.

+6.7% LLM-based EM improvement
−10.5% Average retrievals vs. SOTA
arXiv:2603.12396
